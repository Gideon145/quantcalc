What is a Solana PnL calculator?

A Solana PnL (Profit and Loss) calculator is a tool that helps traders calculate their real profit or loss on Solana token trades. It accounts for entry price, exit price, position size, and all trading fees (buy fees, sell fees, and transaction costs) to give you an accurate net return calculation.
